Uncertainty & Mortality in Covid-19 & Other Diseases

Data Overview

The data used for this visual analytic system comes from the official CDC. The data set used is the "Provisional COVID-19 Deaths by Sex and Age" which holds data from the start of 2020 and was last updated in September 2023. Since the dataset is extremely large the data used for the visualizations has been filtered to data representing the whole United States rather than individual states.
